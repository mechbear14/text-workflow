Google Launches a Free Course on Artificial Intelligence: Sign Up for Its New "Machine Learning Crash Course" | Open Culture


As part of an effort tomake Artificial Intelligence more comprehensible to the broader public, Google has created an educational websiteLearn with Google AI, which includes, among other things, a new online course calledMachine Learning Crash Course. The course provides "exercises, interactive visualizations, and instructional videos that anyone can use to learn and practice [Machine Learning] concepts." To date,more than 18,000 Googlers have enrolled in the course. And now it's available for everyone, everywhere. You can supplement it with other AI courses found in the Relateds below.
